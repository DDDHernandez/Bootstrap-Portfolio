About Me



Hi, my name is Dylan Hernandez, I am a student at the University of Kansas in their Coding BootCamp. I took up Coding as my career of choice after a friend suggested it to me. I have currently completed five out of the six months in the course and I am currently living in Blue Springs with my girlfriend Meredith and our dog, Luella.

Academically, I feel more comfortable on front-end development, but like learning back-end development. I’d like my career to revolve around coding, which I fondly call “the art of logging errors”, for I believe in the power of console.log(ing) to double check each line of code works. Coding is a unique skill that anyone can learn, but takes lots of practice and patience.

I try to dedicate my free time to learning new code from online resources, my classmates, and professionals in the industry. I also like to practice with small coding projects and I am always open to new ones.
